About General Charts

General Charts provide a flexible charting tool for creating various types of graphs from your data. This tool allows you to input X and Y values to create different visualization types.

What it creates: Custom charts and graphs based on your X-Y data pairs, supporting multiple chart types like line charts, scatter plots, bar charts, and more.

When to use: When you need to quickly visualize relationships between two variables or create custom charts not covered by specialized control charts.
