About NP-Chart

An NP-Chart (Number Defective Chart) is a type of control chart used to monitor the number of defective items in samples of constant size. It's similar to a P-Chart but tracks counts rather than proportions.

What it measures: The actual number of nonconforming or defective items in each sample of fixed size.

When to use: When sample sizes are constant and you want to monitor the number of defectives rather than the proportion defective.
