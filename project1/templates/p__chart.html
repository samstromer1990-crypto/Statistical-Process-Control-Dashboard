About P-Chart

A P-Chart (Proportion Chart) is a type of control chart used to monitor the proportion or percentage of defective items in samples. Unlike C-Charts, P-Charts can handle varying sample sizes.

What it measures: The proportion of nonconforming or defective items in each sample, expressed as a percentage or fraction.

When to use: When monitoring the fraction defective in samples of varying size, or when the process involves go/no-go type inspections.
