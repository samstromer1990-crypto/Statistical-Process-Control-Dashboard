About R-Chart

An R-Chart (Range Chart) is a type of control chart used to monitor the variability (spread) of a process. It measures the range (difference between highest and lowest values) within each subgroup.

What it measures: The dispersion or variability within subgroups, showing how much the process varies from one sample to another.

When to use: Used alongside X̄-Charts to monitor both the mean and variability of a process simultaneously.
