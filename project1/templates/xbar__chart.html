About X̄-Chart

An X̄-Chart (X-bar Chart) is a type of control chart used to monitor the mean (average) of a process over time. It tracks variables data and is typically used with an R-Chart or S-Chart.

What it measures: The central tendency (mean) of a process, showing how the average value changes over time.

When to use: When monitoring continuous measurement data to detect shifts in the process mean or average.
